Fellowship Service

Welcome to our Christian Fellowship Service — a space for worship, teaching, prayer, and encouragement. We gather to grow in faith, support one another, and honour God together.

Time: Sundays from 8PM to 10PM SAST

Platform: TikTok Live on the Ninja Talks account

What Happens in Fellowship?

  • Praise & Worship: Songs that uplift and honour God.
  • Weekly Topics: Faith, healing, forgiveness, identity, purpose, relationships, and more.
  • Guests: Different guests join to share testimonies and teachings.
  • Prayer: We pray for viewers, families, and needs shared during the live.
