AI Music Night – Live Top 10

Every Friday we host AI Music Night, where artists submit their AI-assisted or AI-inspired tracks. The community listens live, votes, and the Top 10 are revealed.

Time: Fridays from 8PM to 12AM SAST

Platform: TikTok Live on the Ninja Talks account

How It Works

  1. Submit your track: Artists submit their song using the Google Form.
  2. Live show: All songs are played during the Friday live.
  3. Voting: Viewers vote using the official Google Form.
  4. Top 10: The 10 tracks with the most valid votes become the weekly Top 10.

Submission Rules

  • One submission per artist.
  • You must own the rights to the track.
  • By submitting, you agree that Ninja Talks may:
    • Play your track live on TikTok
    • Upload it to the Ninja Talks YouTube channel
    • Share it on Ninja Talks social media
  • Artists keep full rights to their music.

Voting Rules

  • One vote per person.
  • Voting only counts through the official Google Form.
  • Duplicate or suspicious votes may be removed.
