AI Music Night

This is where artists get to showcase their AI-assisted or AI-inspired music. We listen live, the community votes, and the Top 10 tracks are played on stream.

AI Music Night is about creativity, innovation, and giving artists a platform to be heard.

How It Works

  1. Submit your track: Artists send in one song (AI-assisted, AI-generated, or AI-enhanced).
  2. We review & queue: Tracks are added to the playlist for the next AI Music Night.
  3. Live listening: We play the songs live on TikTok during the Friday stream.
  4. Community voting: Viewers vote for their favourite tracks.
  5. Top 10: The 10 highest-voted songs are highlighted as that week’s Top 10.

Submission Rules

  • One submission per artist: Each artist may submit only one track per week.
  • Original or AI-assisted: You must have the rights to the track you submit.
  • Clean content: No hate speech or explicit content that violates platform rules.
  • Rights: You keep full rights to your song. By submitting, you give Ninja Talks permission to:
    • Play your track live on TikTok
    • Upload it to the Ninja Talks YouTube channel
    • Share it on Ninja Talks social media channels

Voting Rules

  • One vote per person: Each viewer may vote once per AI Music Night.
  • Fair play: No spam voting, fake accounts, or manipulation.
  • Community choice: The Top 10 is based on the community’s votes.

When It Happens

AI Music Night is live every Friday from 8PM to 12AM SAST on TikTok.

The Top 10 from each week are celebrated and may be featured again in future streams or playlists.

How to Submit & Where to Vote

How to submit: Use the details on the Contact page to send your track link and artist name. (Email details will be added soon.)

Where to vote: Voting happens live during the TikTok stream via polls or chat instructions given by the host.
